Who is Narakata?

Narakata is an Indonesian Folk-duo formed in September 2022. The duo is composed of Apriandi Rahman Hakim on guitar and Katleya Talitha Almira on vocal. In January 2023, Narakata released their first single title "Kita yang Terbuang dan Sia-sia".
